FAQ — Where Do I Go For…?

Who do I contact about my NMLS license?
+
Reach out to Rachael Gaines directly — she manages all licensing and compliance tracking for new hires. For urgent NMLS portal issues, call NMLS support at 1-855-665-7123.
Where do I find payroll information?
+
Payroll details and pay stubs are in the HR & Benefits section. For direct questions, contact Janine in HR.
I need IT help — who do I call?
+
Email [email protected] or call ext. 200 during business hours. For Arive or WorkDesk issues, check the Systems tab above for troubleshooting guides.
How do I request marketing materials?
+
Go to the Marketing hub and use the Request Form. Templates for social media, co-marketing flyers, and listing materials are available there.
Who do I talk to about loan escalations?
+
The Operations hub has a full Escalation / Problem Resolution guide. For immediate help, use the Contact Directory to find Kendra or the LOA on your file.
How do I request time off?
+
PTO requests go through the HR portal — find the link in the HR & Benefits section. The PTO & Time-Off Guidelines page covers accrual rates and blackout periods.

How to Complete a COC in Arive
Change of Circumstances — 17 steps
  1. 1
    Go to the loan you need to complete the COC for
  2. 2
    Click Start Editing on the top left-hand side to initiate changes
  3. 3
    Select the reason for the COC
  4. 4
    Make the necessary edits to the loan details
  5. 5
    Click Finish Edits — a summary of changes will appear
  6. 6
    Provide a description of the changes in the text box
  7. 7
    Click Finish & Disclose
    ⚡ Key step
  8. 8
    Click on the Disclosure Forms tab
  9. 9
    Select "Change of Circumstances" from the dropdown
  10. 10
    Click Generate Docs to produce the COC documents
  11. 11
    Review the DocMagic audit findings
    Address any errors before proceeding
  12. 12
    If all is in order, click Generate Doc Package
  13. 13
    Click Generate Package to proceed
  14. 14
    Download the package or click Send for eSigning
  15. 15
    Confirm recipients and documents in the pop-up
  16. 16
    Click Send to initiate eSignature
  17. 17
    Monitor eSignature status and resend if necessary
    📧 Check DocMagic for updates
How to Order Final Docs in Arive
10 steps
  1. 1
    Navigate to your loan pipeline and open the specific loan file
  2. 2
    Review the final approval — confirm loan amount, rate, and PITI all match Arive
    ⚠️ Must match before proceeding
  3. 3
    Move the milestone to "Clear to Close"
  4. 4
    Gather from the lender portal: Final Approval, Final 1003, Final 1008, Final AUS, and MI Cert (if applicable)
  5. 5
    Complete OFAC for all borrowers at sanctionssearch.ofac.treas.gov — label in Arive: OFAC – borrower name
  6. 6
    Pull refreshed credit for all borrowers — label: Refreshed Credit
  7. 7
    Go to Loan Center on the left side
  8. 8
    Click "Final Loan Docs" — a box will appear
  9. 9
    Click "Requested"
  10. 10
    Check your inbox — confirmation email sent to you and the closing team
    📧 Confirm receipt
Order & Send the Closing Disclosure
6 steps
  1. 1
    Finalize Loan Details: All fees, terms, and borrower details accurately entered
  2. 2
    Navigate to Closing Section: Loan & Property Info → enter Vesting, Title Info, Email for docs at the bottom
  3. 3
    Order the CD: Loan Center → CD Status → click Requested
    You'll receive a confirmation email
  4. 4
    Ready for CD: Closer sends CD for review. Send to LO → once LO approves, send to borrower
    ⏱ Within current turn times
  5. 5
    Generate the CD: Disclosure Forms → select "Closing Disclosure" → click Generate Package
  6. 6
    Review and Send: Review for accuracy, then send to borrower for signatures

Assigning LP Findings
Freddie Mac Loan Product Advisor — 7 steps
  1. 1
    Log in to Loan Product Advisor. Enter LP AUS Key or Borrower Last Name → click Find Loans
  2. 2
    Click the radio button next to your borrower name
  3. 3
    On the right side, click the "head and shoulders" icon to assign
  4. 4
    Under "Select 3rd Party Company Role" choose "Seller"
  5. 5
    Under "Company Name" choose the lender. Leave "Mortgage Service Provider" blank. Click Assign
    ✓ Success message confirms
  6. 6
    Click Assignment Log → "Assigned By Me" → click the circle next to the loan just assigned
  7. 7
    Click the "paper with ribbon" icon (View Results) → click the printer icon → upload assigned findings to the lender portal
    📄 These are your assigned findings
